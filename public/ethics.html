9.5 Theories in Action

1. Subjective Relativism

Each person has right to exercise and it depends on his will whether he/she wants to exercise.Same goes for KEVIN (chacha) if he wants to exercise or not .The APP will help our community, get fit, track exercise, make new friends

2. Cultural Relativism

The society decides for our health and we need to exercise or not depends on the cultural society , so do society decides for KEVIN (chacha) if he should exercise or not. It’s an APP that improves UFV’s image, and may sell our university more in the community:

3. Divine Command Theory

This app promotes kindness and respect through wellness and self-care. The God decides whether you need to exercise or not , so do for KEVIN (Chacha) the God will decide whether he needs to exercise or not but no Kevin will exercise under the will of god

4. Ethical Egoism

It works in a way that it's the self-interest of the users . It depends upon the self interest of KEVIN (Chacha) like what he gains in return of doing exercise.UFV will be a leader in this field, nothing like this exists – would could then include other universities/colleges

5. Kantianism

KEVIN ought to do execrise as this might bring profits either way moneywise and healthwise thus we act ethically because we ought to — protecting data, promoting honesty, and treating users as ends.We’re so lucky to live in Canada

6. Act Utilitarianism

KEVIN (chacha) should only workout if doing exercise gives him happiness. Each workout or friendship formed increases total happiness in our community.Thus is produces happiness for chacha also

7. Rule Utilitarianism

Now for Chacha the units of happiness is definetly money but while exercising it would be calories burned by Kevin . By following fair fitness and data practices, it ensures the greatest good for the greatest number.

8. Social Contract & Rawls theory of justice

Kevin should share his wealth as he is very rich so that he can help the less rich people .Therfore the app operates with transparency, fairness, and opportunity for all — profits reinvested into UFV students.

9. Virtue Ethics

Our App works with core values like honesty, courage, balance, and community well-being over profit.
